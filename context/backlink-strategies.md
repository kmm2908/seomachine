# Backlink Building: 15 Proven Strategies (2026)

Source: LinksThatRank — Matthew Woodward
URL: https://linksthatrank.com/how-to-get-backlinks/

---

## Why Backlinks Still Matter

Backlinks remain a primary ranking factor for both Google and AI search tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Grok). Pages ranking #1 have 3.8× more backlinks than positions 2–10. **76.1% of pages cited in Google AI Overviews also rank in the top 10 organic results.** Two-thirds of SEOs say backlinks will become *more* important as AI search grows.

Key signals backlinks provide: Authority · Trust · Credibility · Expertise

## The 15 Strategies (ranked by effectiveness)

### 1. Guest Posting
Write free content for other sites in exchange for a backlink. 64.9% of link builders use this as their primary method. Full control over topic, link placement, and anchor text.

**Find opportunities:** Google operators like `[niche] "written by" "how to"`, `[niche] "contributed by" "complete guide"`. Use pages 4–5 of results for easier wins. Avoid "write for us" pages — Google devalues them.

**Pitch:** Offer 3 topic ideas + additional value (social share, newsletter mention, branded graphics).

---

### 2. Link Inserts (Niche Edits)
Ask site owners to add your link into an *existing*, already-indexed article. Faster ranking impact than guest posts because the page already has authority.

**Find opportunities:** Google your target keyword → open top 20–30 results → look for a natural fit. Or use ChatGPT Agent Mode: *"Find 15 prospects for my page [URL] as a link insert opportunity relevant to [keyword]."*

**Pitch:** Reference the specific article, show exactly where your link fits, and why their readers benefit. Offer to write the paragraph for them.

---

### 3. Digital PR & Journalist Outreach
Get brand mentions/links from major news and industry publications. High authority, hard to replicate. Key for AI search visibility.

**Find angles:** Use Google Trends/News to find trending topics → add your own data/story. E.g. for a plumbing business: *"The Most Overpriced Plumbing Fixes in the UK."*

**ChatGPT prompt for ideas:** *"Give me 10 newsworthy digital PR ideas for [niche] — controversial, data-backed, curiosity-sparking."*

**Pitch:** Under 80 words. Introduce yourself → main finding → 2 newsworthy angles → link to post.

---

### 4. Create Linkable Assets
Content that naturally attracts links without outreach. Two types:

- **Statistics Hub Pages** — curated list of 60–80 stats on a topic with sources. Use ChatGPT Deep Research to compile. Target `[topic] + statistics` keywords (100+ monthly searches).
- **Free Tools & Calculators** — build with Claude/ChatGPT in one prompt. Add to an existing ranking page with an embed code for passive link acquisition.

**Pro Tip:** Seed with 3–7 initial backlinks to help the asset rank, then let it earn links naturally.

---

### 5. Broken Link Building
Find broken links on competitor sites and offer your page as a replacement.

**Method:** In Ahrefs → competitor site → Best by Links → filter 404 → sort by referring domains (highest first). Pitch the site owner with a short, helpful email offering your page as a fix.

*66.5% of links to websites have gone dead over the past 9 years.*

---

### 6. Skyscraper Technique 2.0
Find high-backlink competitor content → create something better → outreach to everyone linking to the original.

**2.0 upgrade:** Focus on **search intent**, not just quality. Use ChatGPT: *"What is the search intent behind [keyword]? What format and angle would best satisfy it?"* Then cover topic comprehensively, match intent, add visual elements, optimise for AI/LLMs.

---

### 7. Convert Unlinked Brand Mentions
Find websites that mention your brand without a link and ask them to add one.

**Find them:** Google `"your brand" -site:yourwebsite.com`. Set up Google Alerts for brand name, URL, and product names. Most site owners just forgot — they're usually happy to add the link.

---

### 8. Publish Original Research & Surveys
You own the data → you become the primary source → journalists/writers cite and link to you.

**Method:** Survey 300–500+ people via Google Forms / Typeform / Pollfish (~$0.80–$1.00 per response). Use ChatGPT to design survey questions and extract newsworthy findings. Publish results page with data-backed title (e.g. *"67% of Small Businesses Spend More on TikTok Ads Than Google"*), charts, and methodology.

---

### 9. Resource Page Link Building
Find curated "useful links" pages in your niche and ask to be included.

**Find them:** `inurl:resources + keyword`, `keyword + useful resources`, `keyword + helpful links`. Or ChatGPT Agent: *"Find 15 resource pages relevant to [industry] that I could realistically get a link from."*

**Pitch:** Build relationship first, offer value, then ask. Higher success rate when you offer a collaboration/reciprocal benefit.

---

### 10. Competitor Backlink Analysis & Replication
Reverse-engineer your competitor's best backlinks.

**Method:** Ahrefs → competitor → Backlinks report → filter dofollow, in-content, DR20+, sort by DR high→low → export CSV. For each link, understand *why* they got it (guest post? resource page? interview?) and replicate the same approach.

---

### 11. Podcast & Interview Link Building
Guest on relevant podcasts = 2 links per appearance (show notes + episode page). Builds brand authority at the same time.

**Find podcasts:** ChatGPT: *"Find 25 smaller podcasts in [niche] that feature guest experts — name, URL, guest type."* Target podcasts that publish episode pages on their website (not just show notes).

**Pitch:** Short email referencing a specific episode + what you'd bring to their audience.

---

### 12. Infographic Link Building
Create visuals for complex data/topics → reach out to bloggers/writers who cover that topic and offer it for free in exchange for attribution.

**Tools:** Canva (free templates). Use embed codes (Siege Media generator) on your own pages so others can copy-paste and auto-backlink you.

**Bonus:** Offer to personalise with their brand colours → significantly higher conversion rate.

---

### 13. Testimonial & Social Proof Links
Write genuine testimonials for tools/services you use → they display it on their homepage (often highest-authority page on the site) with a link back to you.

**Pitch:** Tell them their product helped you → offer to write a testimonial → include specific results. When sending it back, casually include: *"Feel free to add my website alongside my name — [url]."*

---

### 14. Reddit & Forum Participation
Reddit ranks strongly in Google and is cited in AI Overviews. Links don't pass authority like they used to but drive real converting traffic.

**Do it right:** Build karma for 30+ days (15+ quality comments) before placing any links. Target Reddit threads that already rank in Google. Be a community member first.

---

### 15. Strategic Link Exchanges (A-B-C)
**Grey area** — use sparingly as a supplement, not primary strategy. Two-way exchanges are easily detected by Google. Three-way (A→B→B→C→C→A) is harder to detect. Only use between relevant, non-competing sites with similar DR.

## Quality Checklist for Any Backlink

- Relevant to your website niche
- DR 20–80+
- Natural in-content placement
- 500+ organic monthly visitors to the linking site
- Human-written content
- Natural anchor text

## Key Stats

- Average link building email response rate: **8.5%** (need ~12 emails per response)
- Average cost to build one quality guest post link manually: **$250–$400**
- Backlinks typically take **3–10 weeks** for initial ranking impact, **3–6 months** for significant improvement
- Link inserts often show impact faster than guest posts

---

## Recommended Approach

Pick **1–2 strategies** and execute them consistently. Quality over quantity. For local businesses like massage therapy clients:
- **Guest posts** on local/wellness blogs
- **Link inserts** on existing ranking wellness/massage content
- **Unlinked brand mentions** (easiest wins)
- **Digital PR** via local news angles (e.g. "Most Googled Massage Questions in Glasgow")
- **Resource pages** on local business directories and wellness resources

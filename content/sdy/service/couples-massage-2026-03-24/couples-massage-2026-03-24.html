Frequently Asked Questions About Couples Massage

What massage styles can we choose for a couples massage?

Each person can choose on their own. At Serendipity, options include Thai massage, Thai oil massage, Swedish massage, hot stone massage, and aromatherapy massage. One of you might want the deeper stretching of a traditional Thai session. The other might prefer the flowing strokes of a Swedish massage. Your therapist will confirm your choices when you arrive.

Do we need to undress for a couples massage in Glasgow?

It depends on the treatment you choose. Traditional Thai massage is done fully clothed in loose, comfortable clothing. Oil-based treatments, including Swedish and Thai oil massage, use draping so only the area being worked on is uncovered. Your therapist will explain what's needed before the session begins.

How long is a couples massage session?

Sessions are available in 60, 90, and 120-minute options. For a first visit, 60 minutes gives a full treatment for both people. Longer sessions allow more time on specific areas of tension or a more extended hot stone treatment.

How will we feel after a couples massage?

Most clients leave feeling deeply relaxed with less tension in the neck, shoulders, and back. Some people feel a little sleepy for an hour or so after. Drinking water helps the body keep releasing tension. Most people find the effects last well into the next day or two.

Is a couples massage suitable for first-timers?

It's a great choice for someone new to massage. Arriving with a familiar person tends to ease any nerves. Your therapist will walk you through everything before you begin. Just let them know it's your first time and they'll adjust the pace and pressure to suit you.

What is the difference between a couples massage and a standard massage?

The quality and technique are the same. The difference is that two people are treated at the same time in the same room, each with their own therapist. It's the shared experience that sets it apart, not any change in the massage itself.

What Glasgow Thai Massage Offers

Glasgow Thai Massage is run by Maliwan Malone, who trained at the Wat Pho Thai Massage School in Bangkok. Wat Pho is the founding institution of traditional Thai massage in Thailand, and Maliwan has been practising for over 20 years. Her daughter Jariya works alongside her. That's two practitioners with deep roots in the same tradition, offering consistent and personalised care session after session.

Online booking is available at glasgowthaimassage.co.uk/booking-form/. You choose your treatment, select a time, and confirm without a phone call. Pricing is published on the website upfront. There are no surprises when you arrive.

The treatment menu at Glasgow Thai Massage covers Traditional Thai Massage, Thai Oil Massage, Thai Aromatherapy Massage, Thai Sports Massage, Thai Facial Massage, Thai Foot Massage, Thai Head Massage, and Thai Stone Massage. Traditional Thai Massage is performed fully clothed using acupressure, assisted stretching, and work along the body's sen energy lines. It's an effective option for anyone carrying neck and shoulder tension from desk work, or lower back pain that hasn't responded to anything else.

## Linking Rules

1. **3–5 internal links per article** — do not over-link; every link should feel natural
2. **Vary anchor text** — do not use the exact same anchor phrase twice in one article
3. **Services first** — always link to the relevant service page on glasgowthaimassage.co.uk when a treatment is mentioned
4. **Blog to blog** — link between blog posts on related topics to build topical depth
5. **Book CTA** — include one link to `https://glasgowthaimassage.co.uk/booking-form/` per article, ideally near a CTA paragraph
6. **No orphan content** — every new article should link to at least one existing article
7. **Cross-domain linking** — linking from this blog site to glasgowthaimassage.co.uk (and vice versa) is encouraged; both are the same brand

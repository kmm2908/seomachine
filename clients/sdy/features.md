## Core Services

### 1. Traditional Thai Massage
- **What it is**: Full-body treatment performed fully clothed. Uses acupressure, assisted stretching, and compression along the body's Sen energy lines. Soft and deep pressure options available.
- **Key benefits**: Releases deep muscle tension, improves flexibility and joint mobility, relieves backache, reduces stress
- **Ideal for**: First-timers, desk workers, those with chronic tension or poor flexibility

### 2. Thai Oil Massage
- **What it is**: Therapeutic massage using oils. Combines flowing strokes with targeted pressure work. Soft and deep pressure options available.
- **Key benefits**: Deep relaxation, improved circulation, muscle recovery
- **Ideal for**: Those who prefer oil-based work; people wanting deep relaxation alongside physical benefit

### 3. Thai Aromatherapy Massage
- **What it is**: Oil massage using therapeutic-grade essential oils selected for their properties (relaxing, energising, or restorative)
- **Key benefits**: All oil massage benefits plus mood and nervous system support from aromatherapy
- **Ideal for**: Stress-related conditions, anxiety, mood support, general wellbeing

### 4. Thai Head Massage
- **What it is**: Targets scalp, neck, and upper shoulders. Soft pressure only.
- **Key benefits**: Relieves tension headaches, neck and shoulder pain, improves circulation to scalp
- **Ideal for**: Office workers, headache sufferers, anyone with upper body tension

### 5. Thai Facial Massage
- **What it is**: Focus on face, head, and neck. Releases tension in jaw, temples, scalp, and upper neck. Soft pressure only.
- **Key benefits**: Tension headache relief, jaw tension, improved circulation, deep relaxation
- **Ideal for**: Headache and migraine sufferers, jaw clenchers, high-stress clients

### 6. Thai Foot Massage
- **What it is**: Reflexology-based treatment covering feet and lower legs. Works reflex points that correspond to organs and body systems. Soft pressure only.
- **Key benefits**: Stimulates circulation, relieves foot fatigue, promotes full-body relaxation
- **Ideal for**: People on their feet all day, those wanting a shorter treatment

### 7. Hot Stone Massage
- **What it is**: Hot basalt stones used in combination with massage technique. Heat penetrates deeper into muscle tissue. Soft pressure only.
- **Key benefits**: Deeper muscle release, improved circulation, profound relaxation
- **Ideal for**: Chronic muscle tightness, those who want deeper work without intense pressure

### 8. Sports Massage
- **What it is**: Targeted treatment for active clients. Addresses compensation patterns, aids recovery, and supports injury prevention. Soft pressure only.
- **Key benefits**: Faster recovery, improved range of motion, reduced injury risk
- **Ideal for**: Runners, gym-goers, cyclists, team sport players, physically active clients

### 9. Swedish Massage
- **What it is**: Classic flowing stroke technique. Soft pressure only.
- **Key benefits**: Full-body relaxation, improved circulation, stress reduction
- **Ideal for**: First-timers, those seeking gentle relaxation, clients sensitive to deeper pressure

## Content Creation Guidelines

When writing about Serendipity:

1. **Lead with the condition or benefit**, not the technique: "If your neck has been stiff for weeks, here's how Thai massage addresses it."
2. **Name the treatment specifically**: "Thai Oil Massage" not just "massage"
3. **Reference signature technique development** when establishing credibility — it is a genuine differentiator
4. **Reference Jariya Malone** (head therapist) in trust signal sections — she is the lead voice of the business
5. **Tie treatments to real life scenarios**: desk workers, athletes, parents, shift workers
6. **Never invent conditions treated or claims about outcomes** that aren't supported by standard massage therapy evidence
